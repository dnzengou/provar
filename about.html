About ProvAR

There is a power imbalance between the producer, the supplier and the consumer: suppliers cannot blindly be trusted by consumers on the origin of products.

ProvAR propose to give that trust back.


Together we initiated the idea during the Re-Hack one day-and-a-half hackathon organized by Unilever UK, and we are now pursuing the journey from proof-of-concept stage to prototyping.
